    Key Features

    Our Core Features

    Our Course Management System is built to simplify learning, and course administration.
    It connects students, lecturers, and institutions on a single platform for effective academic management.

    Student Registration and Management

    Enables the administrator to register new students, manage their personal and academic records, and update enrollment status.

    Course Creation and Allocation

    Allows the admin to create new courses and allocate them to specific departments or academic levels.

    Lecturer Registration and Course Assignment

    Facilitates the on boarding of lecturers into the system and the assignment of relevant courses for teaching and assessment.

    Score Entry and Evaluation

    Provides lecturers with an interface to input student scores and assess performance based on predefined grading criteria.

    Result Computation

    Automatically calculates each student’s grade point average (GPA) and cumulative GPA based on their performance in registered courses.

    Transcript Generation

    Compiles a detailed academic record of a student’s results across all semesters into a printable and downloadable transcript.
