FAQs

Our Course Management System is built to simplify learning, and course administration.
It connects students, lecturers, and institutions on a single platform for effective academic management.

How do I register for a course?

To register for a course, navigate to the "Courses" tab from your dashboard. Select the course you wish to enroll in and click the "Register" button. You will receive a confirmation once the registration is complete.

How can I reset my password?

On the login page, click the "Forgot Password" link. Enter your registered email address, and we will send you a link to reset your password.

Where can I find my assignments and grades?

All your assignments and grades can be found in the "My Courses" section on your dashboard. Select a specific course to view your progress, assignment details, and submitted work.

How do I contact my lecturer?

You can contact your lecturer through the course page. Navigate to the specific course and look for the "Lecturer" or "Contact" section. This will typically provide an email link or a direct messaging option within the platform.

Can I access course materials on a mobile device?

Yes, our platform is fully responsive and optimized for mobile use. You can access all course materials, submit assignments, and view your grades from any tablet or smartphone browser without needing a separate app.

What should I do if I am experiencing a technical issue?

If you encounter a technical problem, please try clearing your browser's cache and cookies first. If the issue persists, contact our support team at **support@yourdomain.com** with a detailed description of the problem and a screenshot if possible. This will help us resolve it quickly.

How do I submit an assignment?

To submit an assignment, go to the specific course page and find the "Assignments" section. Select the assignment you want to submit and follow the on-screen instructions to upload your file. You'll receive a confirmation email once the submission is complete.
