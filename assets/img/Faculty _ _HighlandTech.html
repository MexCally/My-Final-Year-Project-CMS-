Our Faculty

The highland Advantage

Combining a rigorous academic regimen, life-changing leadership training, entreprenuership skills acquisition, and a hands-on approach to all lecture delivery, Highland College of Technology provides a laser-like clear career development path for all her students.

We draw our faculty strength from both the academia and the industry. Our Faculty pool a cumulative academic experience, practical industry experience and vocational instructional experience spanning 3 continents and of over 2 century's worth. This ensures that our students have access to the best Human Capital Resources.
